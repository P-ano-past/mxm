About Me

Hello, my name is Marc! My past career has involved desktop support and am now transitioning into Web Development. I've studied for a little over a year on JavaScript, HTML, and CSS before joining a coding camp in January of 2020. In the era of MySpace when everyone was able to modify their profiles, I was one of the people constantly changing various lines of code, paying close attention to what broke and what worked.

Now I'm one of the people making the web pages for people to tinker around with! Although I've started a bit late, I'm happy to research and apply my findings to see a project come to life. In the future I hope to see people enjoying what I've created for them. Even if they don't know it was me that created it!
